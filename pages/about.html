Fashion:


I was somewhat into clothing when I was 10-11, during the peak of the supreme/yeezy hypebeast era. I would always scroll through StockX after school and look at all of the different shoes and other clothing items. I was young though, so of course I didn't really have money to buy anything I wanted. Eventually, I just kind of stopped for whatever reason.


Around November 2021 is when I discovered r/FashionReps. I got super into it and eventually ordered some things in late December. To this day I'm still pretty active within replica communities, even though I don't really buy reps that much at the moment.


Since then, I've started to use Grailed, Depop, Buyee, and similar sites often. Thrifting has been another thing I've done at least once every 2-4 weeks for the past year or so. Some of my favorite pieces are things I got from thrift stores for $3-10!


Some of my favorite brands are:


  • Stussy
  • Bode
  • Golf Wang
  • Patagonia
  • Kapital
  • Arc'teryx
  • And many, many, more!

I've also started a small sidehustle reselling clothing that has allowed me to fund this addiction of mine. I mainly sell things on Grailed and Discord. Eventually I want to make a clothing shop website for fun to further my web development skills.


I've made a handful of friends through this hobby that I'm so thankful for as well!!!
